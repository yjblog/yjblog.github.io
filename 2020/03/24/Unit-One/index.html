PoS is Parts of Speech

Words
PoS
 Phrase matching Derivative words
Example
authority n. 权力、权威、专家 1. in authority
持有权力
2. one leading authority
有权威的人		 1. authoritative
a.有权威的;
2. authorization
n.授权、批准		 The judge(法官) had no authority to order a second trial(审判).
demonstrate v.论证、说明 1. demonstrate one’s ablility
显示某人的能力		 1. demonstrative
a.感情外露的、指示的		 Please demonstrate how the machine works.
challenge n.挑战;
v.向..挑战		 1. face a challenge
面对挑战
2. take up challenge
接受挑战		 1. challenging
挑战性的,要求高的,艰巨的		 This career offers a challenge.
